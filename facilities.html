Facilities

Surgical care

Our hospital offers comprehensive surgical care facilities designed to provide patients with the highest standards of safety and precision. Equipped with advanced operation theaters, modern instruments, and a team of experienced surgeons, we ensure that every procedure—major or minor—is performed with utmost care and expertise. From pre-surgical evaluation to post-operative recovery, our dedicated staff focuses on delivering compassionate support and effective treatment outcomes for every patient.

We provide a safe, sterile, and patient-centered environment for all types of surgeries, ensuring comfort and confidence at every step. With modern operation theaters, continuous monitoring systems, and dedicated post-surgery care, we focus on faster recovery and the best possible results for every patient.

     Advanced operation theaters equipped with modern surgical technology.
     Experienced surgeons ensuring safe and precise procedures.
      Comprehensive pre- and post-operative care for faster recovery.

Ultrasonography

Our Ultrasonography facilities are equipped with the latest imaging technology to deliver accurate and detailed diagnostic results. With high-resolution ultrasound machines and expert radiologists, we ensure safe, non-invasive, and reliable imaging for various medical conditions.

We provide a comfortable environment for patients, ensuring quick and efficient scanning procedures. Whether for routine health check-ups, pregnancy scans, or specialized imaging, our team focuses on delivering timely results with the highest standards of quality and professionalism.

     Advanced ultrasound equipment for clear, detailed imaging.
     Expert radiologists ensuring accurate diagnosis.
     Safe, comfortable, and patient-friendly scanning experience.

Uroflowmetry

Uroflowmetry facility offers accurate assessment of urinary flow to help diagnose and manage various urological conditions. Using advanced computerized equipment, we measure the rate and volume of urine flow to evaluate bladder and urethral function with precision and ease.

The test is quick, non-invasive, and performed in a comfortable and private environment under expert supervision. It helps doctors identify issues such as urinary obstruction, weak bladder muscles, or prostate-related problems for effective treatment planning.

     Advanced equipment for precise urinary flow analysis.
      Quick, safe, and non-invasive diagnostic procedure.
      Helps detect bladder and prostate-related disorders accurately.

Emergency care

Equipped with modern monitoring systems, advanced life-support equipment, and a dedicated ambulance service, we are prepared to handle all types of medical emergencies efficiently and with compassion. Every second counts, and our team is committed to providing swift, safe, and expert care when it matters most.

     24/7 emergency services with skilled medical professionals.
      Equipped with advanced life-saving technology and ambulance support.

ICU

Our Intensive Care Unit (ICU) is designed to provide continuous monitoring and advanced treatment for critically ill patients. With state-of-the-art equipment and a team of highly trained doctors, nurses, and specialists, we ensure round-the-clock medical attention and personalized care for every patient.

The ICU is equipped with modern ventilators, cardiac monitors, and life-support systems to handle emergencies effectively. Our focus is on maintaining patient safety, stabilizing critical conditions, and supporting faster recovery through expert medical supervision.

      24/7 critical care with advanced monitoring systems.
      Expert doctors and nurses providing constant supervision.
      Equipped with modern ventilators and life-support facilities.

Laboratory
